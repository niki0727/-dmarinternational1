Services

Independent support across the campaign lifecycle — documents, engineering review and offshore attendance through to approvals and close‑out.

Marine Warranty

  • RAMS & method statement review
  • Lifting, rigging, CoG and motion checks
  • Sea‑fastening, stability and ballasting verification
  • Certificates of Approval (when appointed by client or insurer)

Vessel & DP Assurance

  • Suitability & condition surveys for barges, tugs, HLV/HCV, JUs, semi‑subs, flotels
  • DP capability review, FMEA recommendations, trials attendance

Transport & Installation

  • Jackets, monopiles, TP/OSS, nacelles and modules
  • Route, weather and port interface management
  • Barge moves, towage planning, anchor patterns

Naval Architecture & Temporary Works

  • Intact/damage stability & strength assessments
  • Mooring analyses, seakeeping, and temporary works design checks

Subsea Cables

  • Shallow water methods consistent with DNV‑RP‑0360
  • CBRA approach and repair spreads

Decommissioning

  • Lift and removal planning and verification
  • Waste streams, logistics and port interface

Offshore Marine Warranty & Marine Operations consultancy serving offshore wind, subsea cables and oil & gas. We provide independent review and on‑site attendance so operations run safely and on schedule.
