Sectors

We support developers, EPCs, Tier‑1 contractors and insurers across offshore wind, subsea cables and oil & gas.

Offshore Wind — Fixed

Monopiles, jackets, TP/OSS, WTG installation; heavy lifts and cable campaigns. Interfaces at marshalling yards and ports.

Offshore Wind — Floating

Mooring design reviews, tow‑to‑site and hookup, dynamic cables, station keeping and operations readiness.

Subsea Cables & Interconnectors
