What we do

Clear, standards‑based assurance from planning to close‑out.

Marine Warranty (MWS) – Support

Independent third-party review by professional, registered MWS surveyors covering RAMS/methods, lifting & sea-fastening, stability and motions, with on-site attendance when required.

Vessel & DP Assurance

Suitability and condition surveys; DP capability/footprint and trials attendance; action close-out and readiness checks for safe execution.

Transport & Installation

Engineering review for jackets, monopiles, OSS, nacelles and modules; route and metocean windows; port interface and quayside ops; barge moves and anchor patterns.

Naval Architecture

Intact/damage stability, ballasting sequences, deck strength and motions; temporary works checks; interface with class and flag. Calculations delivered with professional software as needed.

Standards we work to

Our reviews reference internationally used guidance and standards including DNV‑ST‑N001, DNV‑RP‑0360, IEC 61400‑3‑1 and IMCA lifting guidance.
