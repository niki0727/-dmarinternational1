Our approach to sustainability

We apply best‑practice frameworks from globally respected organisations (e.g., DNV, Lloyd’s Register) as guidance for safe operations and risk control. We focus on:

  • Reducing re‑work and fuel use by robust planning and clear go/no‑go criteria
  • Minimising disturbance during near‑shore cable landfalls and port operations
  • Data‑driven lessons learned to improve future campaigns
