Sustainability & our approach

We reduce operational risk and emissions by careful weather window planning, minimising offshore re‑works, optimising tows/transits, and favouring re‑use of sea‑fastening and temporary works.

  • Standards‑led: Reviews reference DNV‑ST‑N001, DNV‑RP‑0360 and IEC 61400‑3‑1.
  • Operational efficiency: Early assurance prevents aborts and standby — less fuel burn and HSE exposure.
  • Material stewardship: Preference for modular, re‑usable gear and documented lifting/rigging plans (IMCA guidance).
  • People & safety: Clear RAMS and toolbox talks that reduce incidents and improve outcomes.
