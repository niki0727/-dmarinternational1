About DMAR International

Hands‑on experience at sea and across the offshore oil & gas, renewables and shipbuilding sectors. Involved at every stage: marine operations, project management, client representation and MWS consultancy.

Core expertise

  • Pipeline and cable laying; installation spreads and anchor patterns
  • On/Off‑hire, suitability surveys and inspections (Jack‑Up, Semi‑Sub, HLV/HCV, tugs, barges, flotels, floating cranes, crew boats)
  • Anchor handling, towing, jacket launching, topsides float‑over, hook‑up, barge moves and positioning
  • Cargo handling, ballast & stability control, tank cleaning and environmental considerations
  • Safety preparedness and training; lifting gear and deck plant maintenance

Selected personal project attendance

  • Sakariya — deepwater pipeline installation
  • Zohr — subsea development
  • Vineyard — offshore wind
  • Kashagan — D‑Island I&T
  • BP ACG (Azeri‑Chirag‑Gunashli)
  • TCO — Tengiz

Project names are presented as personal track record; references available on request.

Professional practice

Our work references DNV‑ST‑N001, DNV‑RP‑0360, IEC 61400‑3‑1 and IMCA lifting guidance. We follow the professional conduct expectations promoted by the Society of Offshore MWS (SOMWS).

Offshore Marine Warranty & Marine Operations consultancy serving offshore wind, subsea cables and oil & gas. We provide independent review and on‑site attendance so operations run safely and on schedule.
